ABOUT THE CONFERENCE Spearheaded by the Ministry of Education, GOI and Indian Institutes of Management (IIMs), the PAN-IIM World Management Conference (WMC) has a profound objective to establish a platform where brilliant minds from India and across the globe converge. This forum fosters meaningful discussions on current management issues, promotes collaborative endeavours, and enriches the existing knowledge reservoir. The Indian Institute of Management Sambalpur is proud to host the 9th PAN-IIM World Management Conference (WMC), which serves as a vital forum for researchers, academics, practitioners, and policymakers to converge. The conference's focus lies on four key areas: entrepreneurial innovation, digital governance, inclusion and sustainability. Over three days, attendees can benefit from publication opportunities, engaging workshops, enlightening plenary sessions, and roundtable discussions with esteemed scholars, policy makers and corporate leaders in the field. The blend of entrepreneurial spirit and technological advancements drives forward-thinking solutions to address global challenges, reducing inequality, empowering marginalized communities, and fostering inclusive growth. Simultaneously, digital governance ensures transparent and efficient decision-making, optimizing resource allocation, and strengthening democratic institutions. This convergence sparks collaborative ecosystems where governments, entrepreneurs, academia, and civil society co-create solutions that drive positive change on a global scale.
