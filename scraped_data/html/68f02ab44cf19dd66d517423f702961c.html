Home Full-Time Faculty



The faculty at IIM Sambalpur represents a diverse mix of academic expertise and industrial experience, creating a vibrant learning ecosystem. They are committed to high-quality research and continuously contribute to advancing management knowledge. In the classroom, their approach blends case studies, experiential learning, and industry insights, making concepts engaging and relevant. They are also known for being accessible and supportive, fostering open dialogue and collaborative learning. Beyond teaching, the faculty actively mentors students, encouraging innovation, critical thinking, and leadership development. Their collective efforts shape a dynamic academic environment that equips learners to excel in a rapidly changing global business landscape.
