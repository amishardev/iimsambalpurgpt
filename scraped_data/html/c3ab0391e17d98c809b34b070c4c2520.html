About the Conference

India's financial landscape is on the brink of a digital revolution. ICMBFT 2025 brings together scholars, industry leaders, and policymakers to explore how frontier technologies such as the Metaverse, Web3, and AI will shape banking, finance, and inclusion toward a Viksit Bharat @ 2047.

Organized by the Indian Institute of Management Sambalpur, this hybrid-format event aims to spark cross-disciplinary dialogue and collaborative innovation for a digitally inclusive future.

Core Themes
Metaverse Banking & Customer Experience
Fintech Innovation, Web3 & Blockchain Ecosystems
Financial Inclusion and Public Digital Infrastructure (DPI)
Cybersecurity, Ethics & Regulation in Virtual Finance
Digital Financial Literacy & Human Capital for 2047
Green Finance, ESG & Tech-enabled Sustainability
