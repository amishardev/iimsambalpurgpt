About The MDP Programme

Indian Institute of Management Sambalpur considers training and development of working professional as an important offering in management education. The Institute offers a wide range of MDPs on futuristic and contemporary themes in all functional areas such as strategy, human resources, marketing, finance, operations, and information technology. All programs are meticulously designed to enrich experiential learning and exposure for participating managers. These programs are a mix of advance learning and application-based management practices.

IIM Sambalpur also offers general management programs for executives shifting into managerial roles at any career stage. Such programs are structured on fundamental understanding of functional areas of management. The participants develop and overall understanding of management and its key interconnected phenomena.

In-company or customized MDPS are training programs designed to cater a specific set of participants with specific requirements. Such programs are designed on need-analysis of client, understanding of industry requirement and according develop training content to simulate an appropriate context. These programs are conducted either in campus or any other location mutually decided in both online and offline format.
