About IMPeC 2025

The conference offers a multitude of events and opportunities, such as seminars, editorial sessions, case study and research paper presentations. The conference also promises thought-provoking keynote speeches from eminent scholars and professionals. The ultimate goal is to improve the quality of research presented by soliciting insightful feedback from experts in a variety of fields. Additionally, attendees will have a better understanding of the problems and trends that their specific sectors of interest are currently facing, which will be beneficial in the future for their research work and the ecosystem as a whole.

This conference is about more than just exchanging information; it is about fostering a global movement of knowledge sharing. By attending IMPeC 2025 conference, attendees will be equipped with the contemporary knowledge, new connections, and motivation to become active participants of academics. The IMPeC 2025 conference would be a testament to the power of collaboration among intellectuals to address the world's most pressing challenges.

Conference Attraction

Focus on Innovation and Emerging Trends

The conference program will feature sessions dedicated to exploring innovative ideas, cutting-edge technologies, and emerging trends in the field. Attendees will have the chance to engage with pioneering research and gain insights into the future directions of the discipline.

Dedicated Doctoral Consortium

A dedicated doctoral consortium will be offered to support the professional development of graduate students and early-career researchers. Opportunities for mentoring, feedback sessions, and networking with senior academics will be provided to foster the next generation of scholars.

Policy and Practice Integration

The conference aims to bridge the gap between policy and practice by facilitating discussions on the implementation of innovative solutions and emerging trends. Panels and workshops will be organized to encourage the exchange of ideas between researchers, policymakers, and industry practitioners.

Inclusive and Diverse Representation

Efforts will be made to ensure inclusive and diverse representation at the conference. Speakers, panelists, and attendees from various backgrounds, disciplines, and geographical regions will be actively encouraged to participate, fostering a rich exchange of perspectives.

Academic Recognition and Publication Opportunities in High Impact Journals

The conference will provide opportunities for academic recognition, networking and publication. Outstanding research papers will be considered for fast-track publication in high-impact journals.

Cultural Immersion

Cultural immersion will be an integral part of the conference experience, with opportunities for attendees to explore the rich cultural heritage of Western part of Odisha, here at Samblapur. Participants will be able to engage in local cultural activities, sample traditional cuisine, and learn about the history and traditions of the region, fostering a deeper appreciation for the host city and its people.

Call for Papers

The IMPeC 2025 Conference invites abstracts and full-length original research from professionals from academic institutions, government agencies, and industry around the world. Each submitted abstract/paper will go through a rigorous peer review process, during which it will be evaluated based on the following criteria: originality, depth of technical and/or research knowledge, accuracy, and relevance to the conference's themes and topics. Paper Submission Guidelines

A maximum of two submissions are allowed per individual. However, an individual can be a co-author in more than 2 papers.

For Abstract Submission:

  • Abstracts must be structured and concise, containing 750 words.
  • The structure should cover research objectives, methodology, and major findings.
  • No citations or references are allowed in the abstract.
  • Formatting should adhere to A4 size, Times New Roman font size 12, and 1.5 line spacing.

For Research Paper Submission :

  • Papers must be original and previously unpublished, reflecting the conference theme.
  • The word count for the paper should be between 6,000 to 8000 words, including figures, tables, and references (including fig, table, references).
  • The paper should follow the suggested structure: Introduction, Review of Literature, Research Gap Identification, Methodology, Findings, Implications, Limitations and Further Research Agenda.
  • Formatting requirements include A4 size, Times New Roman font size 12, and 1.5 line spacing.
  • References should be cited using APA (7th edition) style.
