Director’s Message

Nestled alongside the Mahanadi River in the culturally rich region of Western Odisha, IIM Sambalpur thrives on its commitment to cultivating responsible leaders with entrepreneurial mindsets. Driven by our Vision and Mission, and rooted by the Core Values of Innovation, Integrity, and Inclusiveness, IIM Sambalpur is committed to integrating intellectual rigor with practical relevance.

Since its inception in 2015, with the foundation stone laid and the institute inaugurated by Hon’ble Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi, IIM Sambalpur has rapidly grown into one of India’s most dynamic and future-oriented management institutes. Our flagship offerings including the, MBA, MBA in Business Analytics, Bachelor of Science (Data science &Artificial Intelligence and Management & Public policy) Program, Executive MBA, Ph.D. Program, Ph.D. for working professional and Dual degree Ph.D. program (Executive Ph.D and DBA) are meticulously designed to nurture adaptability, innovation, and responsible leadership.
