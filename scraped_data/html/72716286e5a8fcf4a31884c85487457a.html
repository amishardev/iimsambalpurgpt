International MoUs

MOU Between IIM Sambalpur and Copenhagen Business School was established in 1917 and became integrated as an institution of higher education in the Danish education system in 1965. It is one of the largest business schools in Europe offering world-class research-based degree programs. The institute is Triple crown accredited with EQUIS- AMBA- AACSB.

MOU Between IIM Sambalpur and American Business School ParisWith a curriculum based on the American education system and degrees recognized in the US and France, the American Business School, Paris aims to train its students to become future business leaders who think internationally. An acclaimed institute with multiple awards with geting “Top 2” and “Top 3” best college in France for MBA and BBA respectively. It also received the best intercultural business school in 2019 with L’etudiant.


MOU between IIM Sambalpur and Woosong University was Established in 1954 and is ranked 53rd in the country. Its B-school is the youngest business school AACSB’s History to receive this Accreditation.



MOU between IIM Sambalpur and Alba Graduate Business School Accredited by the New England Commission of higher Education (NECHE), it got AMBA (Association of MBAs) Accreditation. Its Msc in Finance has been Awarded a CFA program Partner Status by CFA institute.

MOU between IIM Sambalpur and Frankfurt School of Finance & Management Frankfurt School of Finance & Management is a research-led business school accredited by AACSB, AMBA and EQUIS. It offers a wide variety of academic and educational programs in finance, economics and management. This includes bachelor’s and master’s degrees, various MBAs, a doctoral program, executive education, study tours and international modules as well as summer schools.

MOU Between IIM Sambalpur and IDRAC Business School, France, one of the top French business schools imparting quality education across the globe since 1965. The institute has campuses in 9 cities around France and is accredited by the French Ministry of education with the membership of AACSB and EFMD.

MOU Between IIM Sambalpur and HEM Business School Founded in 1988, located in Tangier, Morocco, North Africa, HEM is a private Moroccan institution of higher education of management, training, and research. The Institute is accredited by EFMD, AACSB, AABS, and PRME and holds a country Rank of 15.

MoU Signed by IIM Sambalpur with CTBC Business School Originally established in August 2000 as Hsing-Kuo University, later in June 2015 the CTBC Financial Holding took over the university and renamed it as CTBC Business School. It is a current member of AACSB.

MOU Between IIM Sambalpur and Rennes School of BusinessFounded in 1990, ESC Renne is a Grand Ecole, Triple crown accredited institute (EQUIS-AMBA-AACSB) based in France. In addition to its Master in Management, the school awards diplomas in management Doctor of Business Administration (DBA) degree.

MOU Between IIM Sambalpur and Rushford Business School, Business School of the James Lind Institute is headquartered in Geneva, Switzerland. It is a Swiss EduQua certified institution and an ACBSP and IACBE member and ranks in top 10 management institutes in Switzerland.

MOU between IIM Sambalpur and Antwerp Management School Established in 1959, it has a legacy of 60 Years.Antwerp Management School accredited by AMBA, AACSB and NVAO, has been at the forefront of innovation, transformation, entrepreneurship and ecosystems, always in the best interest of people and society.

MOU Between IIM Sambalpur and VUM (Varna University of Management) has been developing leaders since 1996. The university has 160 academic partners and is ranked 9th in Eastern Europe for its MBA degree by Best-masters.com and 1st place among 1600 HEI worldwide for Students Mobility, according to the prestigious U-Multirank ranks.

MOU Between IIM Sambalpur and Luxembourg School of Business established in 2014, is a premier institution offering globally recognized MBA programs, executive education, and specialized master’s degrees. Located in Luxembourg, a key European financial hub, it combines academic rigor with practical insights, leveraging an international faculty and strong corporate ties. Accredited by Luxembourg’s Ministry of Higher Education, LSB prepares professionals for global business challenges through innovative and flexible programs.

MOU Between IIM Sambalpur and Rushford Business School, Business School of the James Lind Institute is headquartered in Geneva, Switzerland. It is a Swiss EduQua certified institution and an ACBSP and IACBE member and ranks in top 10 management institutes in Switzerland.

MOU Between IIM Sambalpur and The University of Essex, founded in 1964, is a renowned research-intensive institution located in England, known for its innovative teaching and vibrant campus life. Offering a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and research programs, it excels in fields like social sciences, business, and computer science. With a diverse international community and a strong focus on impactful research, Essex ranks among the top universities globally for academic and student satisfaction.

MOU Between IIM Sambalpur and Brunel University London, established in 1966, is a dynamic university known for its focus on innovation, industry connections, and practical education. Located in Uxbridge, West London, it offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and research programs across engineering, business, arts, and health sciences. Renowned for its emphasis on employability, Brunel integrates work placements and global perspectives, fostering a diverse and inclusive academic community.

MOU Between IIM Sambalpur and Polimi Graduate School of Management (Polimi GSoM), part of Politecnico di Milano, is a leading business school in Italy renowned for its innovation and excellence in management education. Offering a wide range of MBA, Executive MBA, and specialized master’s programs, it combines academic rigor with practical industry engagement. With a strong focus on sustainability and digital transformation, Polimi GSoM prepares global leaders through cutting-edge research, international partnerships, and a vibrant learning environment.

MOU Between IIM Sambalpur and Asian Institute of Technology (AIT), established in 1959 in Thailand, is a prestigious institution dedicated to advancing technology, engineering, and management education in Asia. Renowned for its international community and cutting-edge research, AIT offers master's, doctoral, and executive programs with a strong focus on sustainability and innovation. Its industry connections and interdisciplinary approach prepare graduates to address global challenges and drive impactful development.

MOU Signed By IIM Sambalpur With Ministry Of MSME, Vedanta Limited, And ICSI, Delhi

For collaborative work, IIM Sambalpur has signed three Memorandum of Understandings and partnered with the Ministry of Micro Small and Medium Enterprises (MSME), Vedanta Ltd., and The Institute of Company Secretaries of India (ICSI), Delhi. Under the scope of MoU, the collaboration has been made in a number of areas which include award to the toppers of MBA Programme, offering live projects to the students, setting up incubation centre, collaborative research work, consulting work, organizing joint seminar and conference, etc. These initiatives will certainly enrich IIM Sambalpur to achieve its vision.

IIM Sambalpur Business Incubation Centre

IIM Sambalpur hosted a national conclave on the topic “Innovation Based Entrepreneurship in MSME – Service Sector “, on the 29th of Feb, 2020. The business incubation centre of IIM Sambalpur was also inaugurated on the same day, as part of the MoU which the institute signed with the Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSME). The incubation centre is set-up with the objective of promoting entrepreneurship and start-ups in & around Odisha.

The proposed centre will incubate small entrepreneurs from rural and semi-urban areas, e.g weavers, artisans, and give ideas to them on use of digital technology to boost their start-ups and business. The centre will mentor them to make their business models a success. The following will be the activities of the Business Incubator (BI):

  • Nucleation of new innovative ideas by creating the environment and opportunities for the know-how providers, entrepreneurs and financiers to meet each other and form business teams;
  • Nurturing businesses in their start-up phase by offering not only space but also access to technology support, business mentoring, networks, scientific and information resources, and a generally conducive and supportive environment, and
  • Promoting and running an active programme for identification, creation, acceleration, and translation (into practice) of innovative ideas suitable for new venture creation.

The BI will be run by dedicated staff that would include faculty, staff, and students of the Institute or experts from outside the institute.
