###### Ethos
Every fest is a culmination of all the emotions a student has experienced in an year. This 3 day event not only completely immerses us into itself but opens the gate to tonnes of memories we’ve, to look back to.
Ethos is just not a fest, it is an emotion, these are those 3 days, behind which the hardwork of a whole year gets put through.
From scintillating cultural and sports events to enlightening management events, the essence of everything is captured here.
It is the best time to forget everything and just let yourself loose in the breeze of fun.
We at Ethos handle promotions, secure sponsorships, ideate the aesthetics, give birth to the theme, share experiences, connect with each other, network with the outside world and what not!
The ideal fest is not which crosses the extent of grandeur but the one which binds everyone’s emotions together and Ethos is an perfect example of it.
