## Bachelor of Science (B.S.) in Management and Public Policy
The Bachelor of Science (B.S.) in Management and Public Policy at IIM Sambalpur is a unique and pioneering undergraduate programme aimed at developing future-ready leaders who understand both business dynamics and the intricacies of public governance. Integrating management science with policy and societal impact, the programme blends interdisciplinary learning with experiential pedagogy. Designed in line with NEP 2020 guidelines, this four-year degree offers exit options after each year and enables students to explore the intersections of corporate strategy, policy design, law, economics, and sustainability. Graduates will be well-equipped to contribute across sectors including government, private enterprise, non-profits, and global institutions. 
### Program Objectives
  * Foster ethical, analytical, and responsible leadership
  * Build competence in management and policy design.
  * Encourage innovation in solving social, economic, and public challenges.
  * Provide real-world exposure through internships, simulations, and research


### Key Highlights
  * Curriculum aligned with the latest industry trends and academic research frontiers.
  * Interdisciplinary degree combining management, public policy, and sustainability.
  * 170-credit curriculum with capstone project and final-year dissertation.
  * Courses span marketing, economics, finance, operations, and analytics with a strong foundation in sustainability and policy.
  * Real-world exposure through internships with NGOs, corporates, and government bodies.
  * Personalized skill development through workshops on critical thinking, design, simulations, and personal branding.
  * Capstone project focused on a business plan or public policy memo.
  * Electives in areas like health, energy, supply chain, and platform businesses.
  * Dedicated research upskilling focus in the final year with dissertation.
  * Courses on emerging technologies, AI, and behavioural economics.
  * Entry-exit flexibility with certificate, diploma, degree, and honours options.
  * Active student participation in clubs, conclaves, cultural events, and entrepreneurship cells.
  * Strong industry and policy interface through conclaves and guest lectures.
  * Multiple exit options in line with NEP: Certificate (1st Year), Diploma (2nd Year), Degree (3rd Year), Honours (4th Year).
