Nestled alongside the Mahanadi River in the culturally rich region of Western Odisha, IIM Sambalpur thrives on its commitment to cultivating responsible leaders with entrepreneurial mindsets. Driven by our Vision and Mission, and rooted by the Core Values of Innovation, Integrity, and Inclusiveness, IIM Sambalpur is committed to integrating intellectual rigor with practical relevance.
