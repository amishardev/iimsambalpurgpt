**5)_Post- Doctoral Research Fellowship Program_ :**
**(A)Essential Qualification:**
IIM Sambalpur offers post-doctoral fellowships to candidates with strong academic credentials, demonstrated research potential, and curiosity to pursue original research. The following are the minimum eligibility criteria.
  * Candidates who have obtained a Ph.D. degree (or equivalent) from institutions of national repute or an equivalent level in such other globally recognized Indian or foreign institutions of comparable standards as may be decided by IIM Sambalpur.
  * Those who have submitted their thesis for a Ph.D. degree may also apply. However, candidates are expected to complete the Ph.D. program and submit a provisional degree or an original Ph.D. certificate within six months of joining PDRF.
  * The candidate must have at least one research paper accepted in an ABDC/ABS category journal.
  * Applicants must have a consistently good academic record and demonstrated research potential.
  * Applicants must have a consistently good academic record and demonstrated research potential.


**(B) Selection Process:**
  * The eligible applications are evaluated based on candidates’ demonstrated research potential and fit with the research interests of IIM Sambalpur faculty members.
  * The shortlisted candidates will be invited to present their research work/research interests/research proposal before the selection committee. A personal interview will follow the research presentation.
  * The institute will offer a Post-Doctoral Fellowship to the selected candidates.


**(C)_Candidates can apply to the following areas of specialization_ :**
  * Finance & Accounting Management
  * OB/HR
  * Information Systems Management
  * Marketing Management
  * Strategic Management
  * Operations Management
  * General Management / Public Policy
  * Economics
  * Business Communication


**(D)****_Structure of the Program_ :**
  * The fellowship will be two years, extendable for another year, subject to satisfactory performance. The institute will not extend the fellowship beyond three years.
  * Post-doctoral fellows would undertake their research activities in collaboration with the faculty members of IIM Sambalpur.
  * Apart from the research activities, post-doctoral fellows are expected to assist their research area faculty member(s) in their teaching activities. Such academic assistance is expected to help the fellows gain first-hand teaching experience.
  * The Fellowship Program Committee will meet once in six months to review the research progress of post-doctoral fellows.
  * By the end of two years, a fellow is expected to complete a minimum of two research papers, either in terms of under-reviews or accepted or published in the reputed international journals (with at least category B in ABDC journal rankings).
  * At the end of the two years, the Fellowship Program Committee will review the research contributions of the fellows. Based on the adequacy of the fellow’s research contribution, the committee may recommend the award of a Certificate of Completion, Post-Doctoral Fellowship Program at IIM Sambalpur. If the committee finds that the fellow’s progress is satisfactory, the committee may recommend the extension of the fellowship up to one year.
  * Post-doctoral fellows are also expected to complete the suggested coursework during the program. The coursework requirement is customized to provide the fellows exposure to other functional areas of management along with quantitative/qualitative research methods.
  * The post-doctoral fellows will conform to all the rules and regulations of the institute governing the services of its employees. They are currently vague and will be announced from time to time.
  * The post-doctoral fellows may avail of the leaves per the institute’s faculty leave policy.
  * The institute may terminate the fellowship during the fellowship program, or a post-doctoral fellow may leave the program after giving one month’s notice. No reason for termination needs to be assigned by either party.


**(E) Financial assistance** :
IIM Sambalpur provides limited financial aid to its post-doctoral fellows (from the date of joining). The details of the same are as follows:
  * A consolidated fellowship amount of Rs 70,000 (Rupees Seventy thousand only) per month.
  * 50,000 per year as the contingency grant to cover expenses related to attending conferences, workshops, purchase of books, and relevant research activities on a reimbursement basis upon production of receipts.
  * 1,00,000/- (upper limit) for attending one reputed international conference during their entire tenure with the post-doctoral program, on a reimbursement basis, upon production of receipts. This conference grant can be accessed only after completing the first year of the fellowship program and submitting one joint research paper with IIM Sambalpur faculty member for publication in a reputed journal (at least category B in ABDC journal rankings).
  * Office space will be provided to PDRF with a laptop and printer. They will be issued a unique research library card, enabling them to borrow up to ten books at a time.
  * Personal medical insurance is covered as per the institute’s policy.
