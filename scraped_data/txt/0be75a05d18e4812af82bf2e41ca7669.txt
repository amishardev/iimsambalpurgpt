#  Summer Placement 
###### [IIM Sambalpur Summer Placement Report 2024-2026 ](https://iimsambalpur.ac.in/wp-content/uploads/2025/06/IIM-Sambalpur-Summer-Placement-Report-2024-26.pdf)
IIM Sambalpur has concluded the summer placements for its 10th MBA batch (2024–26), comprising a cohort of 316 students. The number of recruiters visiting the campus increased by a remarkable 48.23%, reflecting the growing industry confidence in the institute. Additionally, there was a noticeable rise in the number of dual offers extended to students during the summer placement cycle.
These exceptional outcomes are a testament to IIM Sambalpur’s innovative pedagogy and strategic location, which together have played a pivotal role in preparing students for real-world success.
The average stipend for the top 10% of the batch stood at ₹1,65,455, while the top 25% secured an average of ₹1,20,266. Female students received an average stipend of ₹52,440, while male students received an average of ₹57,352.
The largest-ever batch participated in this placement cycle, with Sales & Marketing roles attracting nearly 63% of the students, followed by General Management at 13%. Opportunities were also offered in Operations, Product Management, Marketing, and Finance domains. Notable roles included Technology Consultant, Product Management Intern, Human Capital Intern, Market Research Specialist Intern, Human Resources Intern, Wealth Management Intern, among others.
This year’s placement season saw the participation of over 154 recruiters, including 113 first-time recruiters who came in search of top talent. Prominent recruiters included Adani Group, ICICI Bank, Accenture, Gartner, Micron Technology, Nerolac Paints, PwC India, Red Bull, SAP, EY, JSW, State Bank of India, Reserve Bank of India, Zomato, Amul, ADP, and many more.
###### [IIM Sambalpur Summer Placement Report 2022-2024 ](https://iimsambalpur.ac.in/wp-content/themes/iimsambalpur/assets/pdf/IIM-Sambalpur-Summer-Placement-Report-2022-24_6new.pdf)
IIM Sambalpur's 8th MBA batch of 2022-24 has achieved a 100% placement for its outgoing batch of 193 students. These impressive numbers are a testament to the institute's innovative pedagogy and unique location, which have played a crucial role in preparing students for success in the real world.
The highest stipend secured this year is an impressive Rs. 2.00.000. The average stipend of the top 25% batch has also increased significantly, from Rs. 1,20,413 to Rs. 1,35,270. Furthermore, female students have excelled with an average stipend of 69255.57 while male candidates have an average of 63309.9.
The largest batch size of the outgoing cohort participated in the placement, with Sales & Marketing emerging as the most popular sector, attracting over 49.14% of the batch, followed by Operations with 22.29%. The students were also offered prominent roles in Finance, HR and Operations domains. The positions offered include R&D Intern, Management trainee, Summer Intern, social media intern, BD and Policy intern, Team manager, and so on.
This year's placement season witnessed participation from over 50 recruiters, with an increase of 36 first-time recruiters aiming to find the best talent available on campus. The top recruiters include Adani, Vedanta, Aditya Birla, Cognizant, ICICI, EY, Gartner, HDFC, Dice, V-Guard, SBI Life Insurance, RBI, Daimler, Parry Enterprises, and many more.
Prof. Mahadeo Jaiswal, Director IIM Sambalpur, expressed his gratitude towards the industry stalwarts for their support and belief in the caliber of students and the institute's unique academic culture. Overall, the outstanding placements of the 2022-24 batch at IIM Sambalpur demonstrate the institute's commitment to providing students with the skills and knowledge needed to succeed in their chosen careers.

###### Summer Internship Report, Batch of 2018-2020
“Indian Institute of Management Sambalpur registered yet another successful season of 100% Summer Internship Placements for its 4th batch of PGP participants. During this placement season, the institute hosted more than 50 companies including many first time recruiters. Our prominent recruiters were Procter & Gamble, Ericsson, Ernst&Young, Aditya Birla Group, Hindalco, AGS, HDFC Bank, BRPL, ITC, Airtel, Bharat Petroleum, State Bank of India, ToneTag among many other companies. The students were offered highly coveted roles across various domains such as BFSI, Sales, Marketing, Market Research, Business Development, Consulting, Operations, and HR.
The average stipend of the batch stood at INR 59,789 with the highest being INR 1,68,000. The average of the top quartile of the batch is INR 1,07,413 while the top 50% of the batch secured an average stipend of INR 83,567.
IIM Sambalpur would like to thank its various stakeholders including the recruiters for reposing faith in the institute, its students and its distinct academic culture. With such strong support and collaboration, we wish to continue our journey of harnessing talent at IIM Sambalpur and providing students with the best of opportunities to excel.
The detailed Summer Placement Report would be available soon.”
