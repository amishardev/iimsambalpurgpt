#  Director’s Message 
![](https://iimsambalpur.ac.in/wp-content/uploads/2023/05/director_iims.jpg)
#### PROF. MAHADEO JAISWAL
Director of IIM Sambalpur  
  
  

Nestled alongside the Mahanadi River in the culturally rich region of Western Odisha, IIM Sambalpur thrives on its commitment to cultivating responsible leaders with entrepreneurial mindsets. Driven by our Vision and Mission, and rooted by the Core Values of Innovation, Integrity, and Inclusiveness, IIM Sambalpur is committed to integrating intellectual rigor with practical relevance. 
Since its inception in 2015, with the foundation stone laid and the institute inaugurated by Hon’ble Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi, IIM Sambalpur has rapidly grown into one of India’s most dynamic and future-oriented management institutes. Our flagship offerings including the, MBA, MBA in Business Analytics, Bachelor of Science (Data science &Artificial Intelligence and Management & Public policy) Program, Executive MBA, Ph.D. Program, Ph.D. for working professional and Dual degree Ph.D. program (Executive Ph.D and DBA) are meticulously designed to nurture adaptability, innovation, and responsible leadership. In addition, the Working Professional Program and a wide range of specialized Management Development Programs provide both emerging and experienced professionals with cutting-edge knowledge and practical skills to thrive in today’s fast-evolving business environment. Notably, IIM Sambalpur is among the first to introduce five pioneering initiatives such as the Master Weaver entrepreneurship development program, gender inclusiveness measures, AI-enabled classroom learning, Dual Degree Ph.D. and DBA programs, and a Bachelor of Science.
At the forefront of academic innovation, the institute has pioneered the use of digital classrooms, flip-class pedagogy, and AI-enabled learning approaches, ensuring a truly modern and interactive education ecosystem. Complementing this is the I-Hub Center, our incubation and entrepreneurship platform, which empowers start-ups and innovators to transform their ideas into impactful business ventures. In addition, our strong emphasis on experiential learning engages students in live industry projects, entrepreneurial initiatives, and collaborative research that sharpen their capabilities as empathetic leaders and creative problem-solvers. Further reinforcing the global outlook, IIM Sambalpur has also signed several international MoUs with leading academic institutions worldwide, fostering collaborative research, student exchange, and cross-cultural learning.
Equally important is our commitment to inclusiveness and societal impact. IIM Sambalpur actively promotes gender diversity, equal opportunities, and community engagement, demonstrating our belief that innovation must be both ethical and equitable. We strive to instil a holistic mindset of sustainable and socially inclusive growth in all the endeavours taken up by all of our stakeholders. I invite all those professionals who wish to indulge in Entrepreneurial Innovation and Management Education through experiential learning to become a part of IIM Sambalpur.
**_"I invite you to explore IIM Sambalpur’s vibrant academic environment—where values, innovation, and resilience converge to shape leaders capable of transforming organizations and societies."_**
