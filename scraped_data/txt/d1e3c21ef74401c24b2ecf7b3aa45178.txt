#  How To Apply 
The candidates are required to apply ONLINE for admission to MBA for working professionals.2023, IIM Sambalpur -Delhi Centre. No hard copy/mail copy of the application is accepted. 
Along with the application supporting documents to be uploaded 
  1. Passport size photo
  2. Copy of Master’s/Bachelor’s mark sheets and certificates
  3. Copy of Govt. issued photo identity proof
  4. Copies of SSC, HSC Mark sheets, and certificates
  5. Relevant work experience certificates
  6. NOC from current employer
  7. NEFT/RTGS transaction slip of Application Fee
  8. Copy of Caste and disability Certificate, if applicable
  9. PwD certificate must be issued by a Govt. Hospital, if applicable


###### Application Fees
  * Please upload the receipt of online payment of the application fee of Rs. 2500/- plus GST (non-refundable) along with your application.


  * Please upload the receipt of online payment of the application fee of Rs. 1000/- plus GST (non-refundable) along with your application.


###### Programme Fees
The Programme Fee is Rs. 14,00,000/- (Rupees Fourteen lakh only). The Enrolment fee is payable at the time of accepting the Institute’s offer of admission. The Institute offers Term-wise course structure and accordingly the term-wise fee is payable before the commencement of a term as shown below:- 
Fee particulars |  Amount (in Rs.)  
---|---  
Enrolment fees |  50,000.00  
Term I fees |  2,25,000.00  
Term II fees |  2,25,000.00  
Term III fees |  2,25,000.00  
Term IV fees |  2,25,000.00  
Term V fees |  2,25,000.00  
Term VI fees |  2,25,000.00  
Security deposit (Refundable) |  10,000.00  
Alumni fees |  10,000.00  
The security deposit is payable at the time of Term I fees. The Alumni fee is payable at the time of Term VI fees. The term fee includes charges for library access, cost of materials/cases/library materials/databases/archives, etc. 
However, for getting the dual degree, the students would be required to bear the additional cost of tuition fee, travel cost, accommodation, meals, etc.
